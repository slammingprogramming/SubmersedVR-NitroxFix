# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project should be a practical, welcoming place for people testing, reporting, documenting, and improving SubmersedVR-NitroxFix.

Examples of helpful behavior:

- Be respectful when discussing bugs, regressions, or support issues.
- Share clear reproduction steps, logs, and setup details.
- Assume incomplete reports are opportunities to ask better questions.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors for unpaid work.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that does not meet these standards, contact the maintainers through the repository's available private channels when possible. If no private channel is available, open a minimal issue asking for maintainer contact without naming people or posting sensitive details publicly.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable moderation actions to keep the project useful and respectful.
